I'm Kaffee, I work at the intersection of open-source software and photography. I tend to start projects in a chaotic burst—moving fast and pushing hard until everything clicks. I’m an electromechanics technician, which shapes the way I approach systems: minimal, direct, and grounded in UNIX and KISS principles.

Lately I’ve been exploring [V8](https://v8.dev/) and the [Node API](https://nodejs.org/api/n-api.html) to build highly optimized tools for **Node**, **Deno**, and **Bun**. Projects like *Mon2tr*, a low-memory HTTP/2 client, and a more efficient text encoder/decoder are part of that work, along with experiments toward a cleaner async/await model. Open-source is my preferred environment; it gives me the freedom to build systems that stay lean, purposeful, and transparent.

- I’m currently building **Hibiscus**, a digital abstract compositor written in C.
